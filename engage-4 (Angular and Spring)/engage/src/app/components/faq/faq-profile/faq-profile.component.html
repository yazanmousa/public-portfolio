Questions about the profile


How do I add an organisation to my profile?
First go to the profile tab in the navbar. After this you can select the 'my organisation' option in your profile. Here you will be prompted to either create an organisation, or find an organisation to which you would like to send a join request.
Why can I not immediately create a project when making a new account?
The functionalities of this application depend on the fact that you have or are part of an organisation. Organisations are linked to projects and have a certain 'Level of engagement'. For more information about level of engagement and the DOE diagram, see the FAQ of this subject
What do these reviews on my profile mean?
It will be possible to give another user a review when you both have at some point worked on the same project. The reviews are based on a given list of attributes which you can score. The average scores and the reviews are then placed on the user's profile.
What is the 'my invitations' tab
This is where all your invitations, be it for a project or an organisation, will appear for you to accept or decline them.
