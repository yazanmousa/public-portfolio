Questions about projects


How do I leave a project?
Simply press 'leave project' at the bottom of the project page
How do I add an organisation to the degree of engagement diagram?
This happens in the edit project page. Click on edit project. Then click on add/edit organisations. In the window that appears, you will be able to select an organisation, and specify the catagory it belongs to, and the level of engagement that it has with your project.
How do I change the roles of my project members?
This happens in the edit project page. First click on 'edit project' at the bottom of the page. Then click on 'Change member roles'. In the window that appears, you will be able to change the roles of all members in your project, not including yourself, because you are the owner.
