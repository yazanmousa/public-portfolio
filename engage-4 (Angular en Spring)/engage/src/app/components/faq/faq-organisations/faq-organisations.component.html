Questions about organisations


How do I make a member of my organisation an Administrator?
This happens in the edit organisation page. First you click on edit organisation on the page of your organisation (under the profile tab). Then when in the edit screen, you can select the button 'change member roles' to make another person administrator, or remove their administrative rights. Changing happens by simply clicking on the current role.
How do I leave an organisation?
Simply press 'leave organisation' at the bottom of the information page of the organisation.
